# Game Jams

## Project Overview
Throughout my studies and professional career, I have actively participated in several Game Jams, developing unique titles under tight constraints. These experiences allowed me to iterate quickly on gameplay mechanics and take on diverse roles within small, agile teams.

## My Role: Generalist Developer & UI Specialist
In these collaborative environments, I focused on creating cohesive experiences by handling:
* **Gameplay Programming:** Developing core mechanics, player controllers, and AI systems.
* **UI/UX Implementation:** Designing and coding responsive menus and HUDs to ensure clear player feedback.
* **Game & Level Design:** Contributing to the conceptual phase, balancing mechanics, and building levels to guide player progression.
* **Audio Integration:** Managing the import and implementation of sound effects and music tracks within the engine.
